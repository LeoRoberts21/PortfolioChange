AGMA

Figma Adobe UI.UX Branding

Project Purpose

A brief challenging me to research previous explorers, select a target audience and create a piece of communication design that captures the content in a new and exciting way. The explorer I decided to base this project around is an agoraphobic photographer called Jacqui Kenney. She travels the world from the comfort of her own home, using google maps. Jacqui Kenney got me thinking about all the others who wish to explore the world but can’t for various reasons.

Thought Process

My outcome was a brand that brings the holiday experience to those unable to travel. I did this by designing a brand that had various touch points. The main touch point was a website (designed on Figma) where users could explore and learn about places around the world. I also designed a package that has things like CDs, cookbooks, card games, and a map that can be scanned, creating an AR experience.
