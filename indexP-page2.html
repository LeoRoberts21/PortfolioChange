Netflix D&AD

Figma Adobe Branding Editorial

Project Purpose

D&AD competition brief. My idea was to design a film festival aiming to get UK audiences to watch and talk about non-English language films and series on Netflix. I wanted to focus on the misconception amongst UK audiences that subtitles ruin films because they are a chore to read, making foreign movies less enjoyable to watch.

Thought Process

All the branding is in different languages, with an English subtitle below. I hoped to make viewers more comfortable with subtitles and break the negative connotations they hold. The festival would travel around the country on wheels. I designed all the branding, a zine, social media marketing and UI mockups.
