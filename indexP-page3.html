FOLKLORE ZINE

Adobe Editorial

Project Purpose

This was a live design brief set by agency B.A.M London. The task was to create a zine about a subject or interest that is close to you. I took this brief as an opportunity to experiment with a range of design techniques and disciplines.

Thought Process

I based the zine around folklore, as this is a subject that has always fascinated me. I wanted it to showcase different tales and characters in an eerie, visually compelling manner.
